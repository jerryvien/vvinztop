Who We Are

Leading Smartphone Spare Parts Supplier

Founded in 2014 as VVINZ TOP MARKETING Enterprise, we became VVINZ TOP CELLSPARE Sdn Bhd in 2018 to meet growing demand. With branches in Sungai Buloh (2014), Rawang (2022), Subang (2024), and Kepong (2025), we serve over 300 repair shops in KL/Selangor.
