Who We Are

Unleashing Potential with Reliable Spare Parts

Founded in 2014 as VVINZ TOP MARKETING Enterprise, we transitioned to VVINZ TOP CELLSPARE Sdn Bhd in 2018 to meet growing demand. With four branches in Sungai Buloh (2014), Rawang (2022), Subang (2024), and Kepong (2025), we aim to expand across Malaysia, inspired by MY DIY and 99speedmarket to bring quality parts to every city center.
