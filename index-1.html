Extensive Inventory

We supply parts for major brands like iPhone, Samsung, Huawei, Oppo, Vivo, Xiaomi, and more, covering LCDs, batteries, charging boards, and full housing.

Rapid Delivery

With branches in Sungai Buloh, Rawang, Subang, and Kepong, we ensure parts reach repair shops in under 30 minutes for quick repairs.

Quality Assurance

Our mid-to-high-end parts, including Kupei batteries, match OEM quality at affordable prices, boosting repair shop confidence.

Who We Are

Unleashing Potential with Reliable Spare Parts

Founded in 2014 as VVINZ TOP MARKETING Enterprise, we transitioned to VVINZ TOP CELLSPARE Sdn Bhd in 2018 to meet growing demand. With four branches in Sungai Buloh (2014), Rawang (2022), Subang (2024), and Kepong (2025), we aim to expand across Malaysia, inspired by MY DIY and 99speedmarket to bring quality parts to every city center.

  • Over 300 repair shop clients in KL/Selangor trust us for consistent supply.
  • Partnered with top brands like Kupei for stable, high-quality components.
